Seraphims of Astraeus is a semi-realistic, part arcade-y, fast paced shooter from a new indie developer. With ample room for customization of your mech including limbs, arsenal, and color, there is something for every mecha fan. Test your mechs against other players heat of battle with Steam and LAN supported multiplayer. If cooperative play is what you're looking for, gather your friends for a dive into the prehistoric world of Pangaea.

Harness the power of a Seraphim mech to soar through the skies with grace and power. Traverse lands burdened by past crises and dance through crosfire in a display of speed and agility, defeating the vessels of war forged by those who dare to tarnish the sanctity of an alternate Pangaea. Stories of conflicts arise between the nations of Astraeus, Nair-Lapierre, and Karmel in a 12 mission episodic-like series, and it's up to you to pilot your way to victory. Are you up to the task?
